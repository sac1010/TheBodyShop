Join Us To Light A Little Life

The pandemic has devastated some of the most vulnerable communities in our country, 30,071 Indian children became vulnerable during the second wave of COVID 19 pandemic. These Children lost their families or were abandoned due to poverty. To support these children The Body Shop India in association with Miracle Foundation India have launched LightALittleLife campaign.

LightALittleLife is a comprehensive program through which we will provide vital education and healthcare support for these children. The program will provide school supplies, coaching & tutor support; internet connectivity for online learning; timely medical tests, prescriptions, supplements; access to quality healthcare inclusive of emergency healthcare. To this end, we are raising funds through all its point of sales including its 200+ retail stores as well as online store at www.thebodyshop.in allowing for voluntary customer donations of minimum INR 20.

So, join us in our endeavour to light a little life.
